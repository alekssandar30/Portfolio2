Something about me



I am an undergraduate student at the Faculty of Technical Sciences, University of Novi Sad (Serbia). Department: Power, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering / Study module: Power Engineering – Power Electronics and Electric Machines.

Before starting the university I had successfully finished Gymnasium in Sremska Mitrovica, Serbia. At the beginning of the third year of university, I was a student demonstrator for the subject ''Object-oriented programming'' where I had the chance to help other students to familiarize and master the aforementioned subject. Also, I have found Matlab Simulink to be a very helpful program for simulation of Power Electronics, even more so if you happen to have previous knowledge in programs such as C, C++, etc.

I am passionate and also experienced in making healthy habits in people's lifestyle which includes writing nutrition and training programs. Currently, I am working in the gym called ''Jump&Pump'' as a Body Forming instructor which has given me the chance to do what interests me: writing online nutrition guides for others, doing personal and also group training. I try to balance that time with time for studying and I am looking for an internship (as a student of the third year) in the area of Modelling and Simulation of Power Electronics. I am very much interested in Automotive Industry and Renewable Energy Sources.
